## ğŸš¨ Problem Statement
Visually impaired individuals face significant challenges in daily navigation:
- Difficulty detecting obstacles at a distance  
- Inability to identify object types (vehicles, stairs, pits, etc.)  
- Unsafe navigation on uneven roads and crowded areas  
- Risk of accidents due to water puddles and potholes  
- Lack of emergency communication during danger  
- Heavy dependence on others for mobility  

Existing smart sticks in the market only provide basic obstacle detection and do not offer intelligent navigation or safety features.

There is a strong need for an affordable, AI-powered, and user-friendly assistive system that enables safe and independent movement.
